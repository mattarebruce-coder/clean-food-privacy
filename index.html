Privacy Policy

PureBite for iOS · Effective July 14, 2026

The short version: PureBite collects no personal data. Ever. There are no accounts, no ads, no trackers, and no analytics. Everything the app learns about your food stays on your iPhone.

What the app does

PureBite scans food barcodes (or accepts a typed barcode or food-name search), looks the product up in public food databases, and scores how natural it is. An optional AI summary of the result is generated on your device.

Information we collect

None. PureBite has no user accounts, no sign-in, and no analytics or advertising SDKs. We do not collect, store, sell, or share any personal information.

Camera

The camera is used only to read product barcodes, and only while you're on the scanning screen. Camera images are processed entirely on your device, are never saved, and are never uploaded anywhere.

Food database lookups

To identify a product, the app sends only the barcode number or the food name you searched to two public, non-commercial food databases:

  • Open Food Facts (a community project)
  • USDA FoodData Central (a U.S. government service)

To make lookups faster, the app also checks a shared product cache we operate — a database containing only food product information (barcodes, ingredients, nutrition), never anything about you or your device. Products you look up may be added to this cache so lookups are faster for everyone.

These requests contain no personal information, no identifiers, and no location data. Like any internet request, the services may see your IP address as a technical necessity; their own privacy policies apply to their handling of requests.

Feature requests

If you choose to submit a feature request, the text you write is sent to our database so it can be reviewed and shown on the in-app request board. Please don't include personal information in what you write. Requests are linked only to a random identifier generated on your device — never to your name, email, or Apple ID — and that identifier exists solely to prevent duplicate votes and spam. Requests are reviewed before they appear publicly, and anyone can report a post for removal.

AI analysis

The AI food analysis is generated by Apple Intelligence entirely on your iPhone. Your food data is never sent to us, to Apple's servers, or to any third-party AI service for this feature.

Scan history

Your recent scans are stored only on your device. Deleting the app deletes them. We have no copy and no way to access them.

Purchases

Subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment details. You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime in your Apple ID settings. Apple's handling of purchase data is described in Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children

PureBite is not directed at children and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Health disclaimer

PureBite's scores and AI summaries are general food-quality information, not medical or nutritional advice. Always confirm ingredient and allergen information on the product's physical label.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this address with a new effective date. Because the app collects nothing, changes are unlikely.
